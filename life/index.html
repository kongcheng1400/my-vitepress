avoid ruin #

WARNING

如果我知道我会死在哪儿，我就不会去那儿。

The one thing you have to avoid is the risk of ruin.

  • stay out of jail
  • stay out of total catastrophic loss
  • stay out of things that could be physically dangerous or hurt your body
  • stay out of things that could cause to lose all of your capital, all of your savings.
  • Don’t gamble everything on one go.

I don’t believe I have the ability to say what is going to work. Rather,I try to eliminate what’s not going to work. I think being successful is just about not making mistakes. It’s not about having correct judgement. It’s about avoiding incorrect judgements.
